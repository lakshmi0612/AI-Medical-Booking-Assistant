# Medical Clinic Information Guide

## About Our Clinic

Welcome to HealthCare Plus Medical Center. We are a comprehensive healthcare facility offering a wide range of medical services to our community.

### Our Services

#### General Consultation
Our general practitioners provide comprehensive primary care services including:
- Annual health checkups
- Common illness diagnosis and treatment
- Preventive care and health screenings
- Vaccination services
- Health counseling and lifestyle advice

**Available**: Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

#### Pediatrics
Our pediatric department specializes in children's healthcare:
- Well-baby checkups
- Childhood vaccinations
- Growth and development monitoring
- Treatment of common childhood illnesses
- Nutritional counseling for children

**Pediatrician Hours**: Monday to Friday, 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM

#### Cardiology
Our cardiology department offers:
- Heart disease screening
- ECG and stress tests
- Blood pressure management
- Cholesterol monitoring
- Heart health consultations

**Cardiologist Available**: Tuesday and Thursday, 2:00 PM - 6:00 PM

#### Dermatology
Skin care services include:
- Acne treatment
- Skin allergy management
- Mole and lesion examination
- Cosmetic dermatology consultations
- Eczema and psoriasis treatment

**Dermatologist Hours**: Wednesday and Friday, 11:00 AM - 4:00 PM

#### Orthopedics
Bone and joint care services:
- Sports injury treatment
- Arthritis management
- Fracture care
- Joint pain diagnosis
- Posture correction advice

**Orthopedist Available**: Monday, Wednesday, Friday, 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM

#### Dental Services
Complete oral healthcare:
- Routine dental checkups
- Teeth cleaning
- Cavity treatment
- Dental X-rays
- Emergency dental care

**Dental Clinic Hours**: Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

## Appointment Guidelines

### Booking Process
1. Choose your preferred service type
2. Provide your contact information
3. Select your preferred date and time
4. Receive email confirmation
5. Arrive 10 minutes before your appointment

### Cancellation Policy
- Cancel at least 24 hours in advance
- Call us or use our online system
- Late cancellations may incur a fee

### What to Bring
- Valid ID proof
- Insurance card (if applicable)
- Previous medical records (if any)
- List of current medications
- Any relevant test results
